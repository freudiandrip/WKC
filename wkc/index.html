2023's Best in Show Finalists

Best of Breed

There are 7 breed groups, each composed of roughly 25-35 breeds. A total of ~210 contenders compete in their respective groups for the Best of Group title. The 7 group winners then advance to the Best in Show Bracket.

Best of Group

There are 7 breed groups, each composed of roughly 25-35 breeds. A total of ~210 contenders compete in their respective groups for the Best of Group title. The 7 group winners then advance to the Best in Show Bracket.

Best in Show

The Best in Show champion is selected out of the 7 group winners. Factors taken into consideration include the level of fidelity the dog holds to the breed standard as well as an element of je ne sais quois that sets apart the champion. Winners include the first place Best in Show, as well as a second place reserve Best in Show.
